# Joplin Tree Service: 20-Post SEO Blog Strategy

**Goal:** Dominate "Informational" search intent to build local authority and funnel traffic to service pages.
**Keyword Strategy:** Hyper-Local (Joplin/Jasper County) + Specific Problem Solving.
